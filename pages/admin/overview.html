Admin's Overview

Welcome, Admin!

Create Subject

Set up new subjects and assign faculty members with this feature. Ensure each subject has a designated instructor and all required parts, such as academic year and year and section

Update Subject

Manage and revise existing subjects with the Update Subject feature. Assign or reassign faculty members, update course description, etc. to ensure the subject remains current and meets educational standards.

Update Course

Keep your academic offerings up-to-date with the Update Course Lists feature. Modify, add, or remove courses such as BSCS or BSIT to reflect the latest curriculum changes.

Update Cohort

Maintain accurate records of the branches and campuses with the Update Cohort Lists feature. Add, modify, or remove cohort details to reflect the current organizational structure.

Update Department

Manage various colleges with the Update Departments feature. Add, edit, or remove department details to keep your academic structure up-to-date.

Enroll Student

Officially enroll students in their designated courses, ensuring they are registered for the correct classes and schedules
